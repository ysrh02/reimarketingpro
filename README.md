## What Is REI Marketing Pro, Exactly?

REI Marketing Pro is a done-for-you marketing management agency built specifically for real estate investors, wholesalers, and house flippers across the USA. They don't sell you software — they *run your marketing system for you*. Their team sets up the campaigns, manages the responses, handles the data, trains and supervises cold callers, and pushes hot leads directly into your CRM.

Founded by Zeeshan Shakeel (a computer science grad turned real estate marketing specialist), the agency has quietly become a go-to resource for investors who are tired of duct-taping together five different tools and still not getting consistent leads.

The services they cover include:

- **SMS Marketing** — platform setup, 10DLC registration, campaign execution, response management, CRM handoff
- **Cold Calling Management** — caller training, script workflows, KPI tracking, DNC management
- **List Pulling & Management** — targeted list sourcing, skiptrace, recycling, CRM upload
- **Ringless Voicemail** — voicemail drops as part of a broader follow-up stack
- **Direct Mail** — targeted campaigns integrated with your SMS and calling efforts

- **Virtual Assistants** — for ongoing ops, CRM updates, and tool management
- **Social Media & Web Development** — for brand presence and investor-focused lead gen sites

One important note: **you keep ownership of all your tools.** REI Marketing Pro manages them on your behalf, but the accounts, data, and assets belong to you. That's a big deal — if you ever part ways, you're not starting from scratch.
